Garibaldi's

Garibaldi's in Apopka dates back to 2000.

Family owned and operated by the Torres Family. We are very blessed to have ended up in the city of Apopka.

Over the years Garibaldi has had the opportunity to establish a large following of loyal customers which we are forever grateful for, without you there would be no us.

Thank you for these 22 years of continued success. Thank you for making Garibaldi's a fun family friendly atmosphere. Come check us out and be part of our legacy.
