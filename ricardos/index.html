Our Story

It’s about family, friends, and food from home.
Long-time Tahoe locals, Jesus “Chuy” Ahumada and Stacey Hernandez, transformed a dream into reality when they opened Sombreros Mexican Restaurant in 2017, giving their prior success with catering a home for everyone to enjoy. Chuy and Stacey share family recipes with the flavors of Ameca, Jalisco, in the delicious tacos, burritos, tortas, and many other Mexican favorites. You'll feel like you’re part of the family cookout with the kids, Melody, Melissa and Jesus Jr, sitting nearby and mama y papa helping in the kitchen.
It’s not just family-owned, but fully family operated!

Mexican Tacos

Three flour tortillas filled with our seasoned, breaded, Camarones Locos. Topped with lettuce, tomatoes, diced avocado and light queso fresco.

Guacamole and Queso

Guacamole made fresh with avocados, tomatoes, onions, and fresh lime juice served alongside our homemade Queso and famous chips.

Enchiladas

Three corn tortillas rolled around a savory filling, then topped with a chile sauce and baked daily.
